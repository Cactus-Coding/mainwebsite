What is Cactus Coding?

We're a community of homeschooled high schoolers passionate about technology and creativity

🚀

Build Projects

Work on real projects that matter to you. From websites to games, apps to art - if you can code it, we'll help you build it.

🤝

Learn Together

No experience required! We learn from each other through workshops, pair programming, and collaborative projects.

🌟

Make Friends

Connect with other homeschooled teens who share your passion for technology in a supportive, creative environment.

🦎
🌵

What We Do

Every meeting is packed with hands-on learning and creative coding

💡

Workshops

Learn web development, Python, game design, and more through interactive workshops led by members and guest speakers.

🎮

Hackathons

Participate in local and online hackathons where you'll build projects in 24-48 hours and compete for prizes.

🎨

Project Time

Work on your personal projects with support from the group. Get feedback, debug together, and celebrate wins.

🍕

Social Events

Pizza parties, game nights, and field trips to local tech companies. Coding is better with friends!

🦂
