Who We Are

Cactus Coding is a Hack Club chapter dedicated to serving homeschooled high school students in Scottsdale, Arizona. We meet regularly to code, collaborate, and create amazing projects together. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced programmer, you'll find a welcoming community here.
